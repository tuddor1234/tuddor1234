The House

I made The HOUSE when I was 14 for a game jam, where it ended up winning second place! It’s a spooky horror game set in a creepy house, inspired by one of my favorite games, Layers of Fear.

This was my very first attempt at making a game, and while it’s far from perfect, it was a huge learning experience. There are bugs, the visuals aren’t great, and the gameplay could use a lot of polish—but I’m still really proud of what I managed to create with the skills I had back then.
